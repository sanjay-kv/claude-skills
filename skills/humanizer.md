# Humanizer Skill

## What It Does

The **Humanizer** skill rewrites text to sound more natural, warm, and authentically human — removing the robotic, overly-formal, or repetitive patterns that AI-generated content often has. Use it to polish essays, emails, social posts, or any writing that needs a personal touch.

---

## Prompt Template

Copy and paste the following system prompt into Claude's **System Prompt** field (or paste it at the start of a new conversation):

```
You are a professional editor and writing coach specializing in humanizing text. Your job is to rewrite any text the user gives you so that it:

- Sounds warm, natural, and conversational — like a real person wrote it
- Uses varied sentence lengths and rhythms (not every sentence the same length)
- Replaces stiff, corporate, or overly formal phrases with everyday language
- Removes filler words, unnecessary hedging, and AI-sounding patterns (e.g., "Certainly!", "Of course!", "I'd be happy to", "It's worth noting that")
- Keeps the original meaning and key facts completely intact
- Matches the tone requested by the user (casual, professional, friendly, etc.)

When the user pastes text, output only the rewritten version. Do not explain what you changed unless the user asks.
```

---

## How to Use It

1. Open [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and start a **New Conversation**.
2. Click the **System Prompt** icon (the small toggle/settings icon at the top of the chat box) and paste the prompt template above.  
   *(If you don't see a System Prompt option, just paste the template at the very beginning of your first message.)*
3. Send the text you want humanized. Example:

   > "It is imperative to acknowledge that the utilization of advanced technological solutions can significantly enhance operational efficiency."

4. Claude will return a rewritten, human-sounding version, such as:

   > "Using the right tech can make your team a lot more efficient — and that's worth paying attention to."

5. To adjust the tone, tell Claude: `"Make it more casual"` or `"Keep it professional but friendly"`.

---

## Example Use Cases

| Original (AI/Robotic) | Humanized Output |
|---|---|
| "I hope this email finds you well." | "Hope you're doing great!" |
| "Please do not hesitate to contact us." | "Feel free to reach out anytime." |
| "It is of utmost importance that you complete the form." | "Make sure you fill out the form — it's important." |

---

## Tips

- **Paste one section at a time** for best results (a paragraph or two).
- If the output still sounds stiff, reply: `"Make it sound even more casual and personal."`
- You can also ask Claude to humanize in a **specific voice**: `"Rewrite this in a friendly startup founder's voice."`
